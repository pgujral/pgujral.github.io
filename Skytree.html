Competence

  • Their CO2 re-capture process was originally developed by ESA to make longer space missions possible by extracting the CO2 exhaled by astronauts on board of spacecraft

  • They built a design to remove CO2 and H2O continuously for up to 4 passengers and keep the CO2 concentration and Relative-Humidity constant over prolonged periods of time.

  • This technology reduces the need for this by ensuring that CO2 levels are maintained at an optimum level which enables the re-circulation of indoor air without bringing in outside air thereby reducing the load on HVAC systems

Their Design

Skytree Twin Module (prototype) consists of two 2L cartridges each equipped with a fan and a heater in the inlet module. The outlet module consists of two flaps which are being automatically controlled allowing for change in air flow direction depending on the mode (adsorption or desorption). The system is designed to remove CO2 continuously.
